About Me

During my short tenure as a programmer, I’ve worked with front-end tools such as AngularJS and taught myself a variety of algorithms. I love designing a technical tool to be as cost-effective as possible. During my free time, I like to run, work on my drawing and print-making, and learn new languages (I’m fluent in English, Mandarin, and Shanghainese, can converse in Spanish, and can read some Latin). Some of my most enjoyable Penn State undergraduate memories are of organizing and participating in different hackathons. From these experiences I’ve developed long-standing friendships and a mind eager for new ideas.
